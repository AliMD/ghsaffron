    History
    Iranian saffron is the most valuable herb that is derived from the slopes of Mount Alvand. Iranian saffron is exported to many parts of the world in ancient times, the properties of the Greeks, Romans, Chinese and the Arabs are Semite cultivation procedures introduced in the first and fourth century AH, the Muslims learned about the Mediterranean. First Saffron fields States World Media (Skinheads and Boroujerd areas of Hamadan and Kermanshah) was established and then go and Qom and Esfahan and some coastal areas of the Caspian Sea and the Persian state expanded. Later planting saffron in Khorasan region and beyond, and finally mass production of high quality saffron Ghaheston (Qaenat) has focus. Production and international trade of these products is always in the hands of the Iranians. Now the world's saffron is produced in Khorasan largest and finest.
